Our Mission

At SafeDrive, we're committed to making roads safer for everyone. Each year, thousands of accidents are caused by driver fatigue, distraction, and impairment. Our mission is to significantly reduce these numbers through advanced AI technology that alerts drivers before dangerous situations develop.

We believe that technology should serve humanity, and what better way to demonstrate this than by saving lives on the road?

Safer Roads for Everyone

Our Technology

Eye Detection

Advanced algorithms track eye movements, blink patterns, and pupil dilation to detect signs of fatigue or distraction.

Facial Emotion Analysis

AI models analyze facial expressions to determine emotional state and alertness levels.

Voice Emotion Detection

Voice pattern analysis detects stress, fatigue, or impairment through speech characteristics.

Yawning Detection

Computer vision identifies yawning frequency as a key indicator of driver fatigue.

Head Position Tracking

Monitors head tilt and position to detect nodding off or lack of attention to the road.

AI-Powered Analysis

Machine learning models continuously improve detection accuracy based on driving patterns.
